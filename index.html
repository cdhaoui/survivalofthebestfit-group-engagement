Machine Bias Exists Everywhere

Is software neutral?

Survival of the Best Fit - Group Engagement Edition is an interactive simulation to explain AI bias in a fun group activity in-class or online using a live group voting mechanism. It is an extended version of the original game Survival of the Best Fit, introducing group dynamics where students scan a QR code to join and start voting using their mobile devices. This helps boost students' engagement by turning the simulation into a collaborative learning experience rather than an individual activity.

Survival of the Best Fit is an educational game about hiring bias in AI. We aim to explain how the misuse of AI can make machines inherit human biases and further inequality. Much of the public debate on AI has presented it as a threat imposed on us, rather than one that we have agency over. We want to change that by helping people understand the technology, and demand more accountability from those building increasingly pervasive software systems.

Group Engagement Edition Built by Dr Chedia Dhaoui based on the original game built by Gabor Csapo, Jihyun Kim, Miha Klasinc, and Alia ElKattan.

Original game supported by the Creative Media Award from Mozilla Foundation.
